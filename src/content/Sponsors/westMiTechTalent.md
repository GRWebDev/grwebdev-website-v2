## Sustaining Sponsor

West Michigan Tech Talent connects local employers, educators, community organizations and workforce experts who are
invested in creating a strong tech workforce. By sharing best practices, leveraging resources and collaborating on
innovative solutions, WMTT is actively addressing the tech talent gap and makes upskilling affordable and accessible.
Learn more at [westmichigantechtalent.com](https://www.westmichigantechtalent.com/).
